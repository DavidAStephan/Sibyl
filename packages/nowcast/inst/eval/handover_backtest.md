## What this measures

For every handover variable present in the bundled MARTIN data fixture (`packages/martin/inst/extdata/martin_data_fixture.xlsx`), the script chops the last 2 quarters off, forecasts 2 quarters ahead with each method, and compares the central forecast against the held-out actuals. The fixture's history ends 2019Q3, so this is a pre-COVID, in-sample-vintage recovery test, not an out-of-sample real-time evaluation.

Variables evaluated: 44. Held-out points per method: up to 88.

## Caveats

- This is a smoke-test-grade backtest on a single frozen fixture, not a forecast-accuracy benchmark. The horizon is short (2 quarters) and the sample is one pre-COVID end-point.
- The `bridge` method here is fable's constrained AR(1)+seasonal-AR(1) ARIMA, not the monthly-indicator `bridge_monthly` (the fixture has no monthly indicators).
- Numbers are whatever the committed code yields on the committed 
  fixture; re-running the script regenerates this file.
